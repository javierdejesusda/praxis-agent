## No Guarantees

Past backtest performance does not guarantee future results. The backtest metrics reported in this repository are based on historical data and may not reflect real-world trading conditions. Specifically:

- **Backtests are hypothetical.** They assume perfect execution at modeled prices and do not account for all real-world frictions (market impact, partial fills, exchange outages, API latency).
- **Markets change.** Strategies that worked historically may fail in the future due to regime shifts, liquidity changes, or structural market evolution.
- **Parameter overfitting.** Despite rigorous in-sample/out-of-sample separation and statistical validation (DSR, Monte Carlo, PSR), the risk of overfitting to historical data is inherent in any systematic strategy.
